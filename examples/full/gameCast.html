Game streaming example

    How to use

  • Enter the channel name and click Create button.
  • If you press 'create' button, 'Screen Share' button will be created.
  • Open the same site on other tab or browser. Then click on channel you created in the channel list.
  • When you press the 'Screen Share' button, you can select the screen to share.
  • If you started sharing, your opponent can see your screen together.
